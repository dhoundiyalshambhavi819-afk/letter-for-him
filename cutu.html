💌 A Secret Letter

Hi, my love ❤️

If you're reading this it means you found my little secret I don't know if I'll ever be able to put this into words properly, but I just wanted you to know something... 🤍 These past few months have changed me in so many ways. There were days when I was scared, overwhelmed, angry, confused, and there were days when I felt like I was losing everything that mattered to me. Through all of that, one thing I realized is how much you truly mean to me. Thank you for staying. Thank you for being patient with me even when my overthinking became too much. I know I haven't always been easy to understand, and I know I've made mistakes too. But thank you for never making me feel like I was impossible to love. Aapko shayad kabhi pata bhi na chale ki kitni baar aapki ek call, ek text, ya sirf ek "m hu na babyy" ne mujhe sambhala hai. Shayad maine kabhi itna clearly bataya bhi nahi, par aap meri life ka bohot important hissa ho. Main jaanti hu hum dono perfect nahi hain. Hum dono se galtiyan hoti hain. Kabhi main hurt ho jaati hu, kabhi aap. Kabhi hum ek dusre ko samajhne me time lete hain. Lekin shayad pyaar ka matlab bhi yehi hai... har misunderstanding ke baad bhi ek dusre ko choose karna. 🤍 Aapke saath maine ye seekha hai ki kabhi kabhi relationship perfect moments se nahi, imperfect logon ke baar baar ek dusre ke paas laut aane se bante hain. Aur ek baat... mujhe aapki woh choti choti baate bohot pasand hain. Jab aap mujhe "mera betuuu", "babyy" ya kisi pyaare nickname se bulate ho na, toh sach me dil khush ho jata hai. Shayad choti baat lage, par mere liye nahi hai. Mujhe un baaton me aapka pyaar feel hota hai. 🥹❤️ I hope no matter how busy life gets, we never stop choosing each other, never stop talking, never stop understanding, and never stop making each other feel loved. I love you. More than I can explain, more than I can express, and probably more than you'll ever know. Thank you for being my safe place, my comfort, and the person I always want to come back to. ❤️🫂 Take care of yourself, okay? Because your happiness, your smile, and even your "haan yaar" matter more to me than you realize. And if there's one thing I'll keep choosing, again and again... it'll always be us. 🤍🌷
❤️ Always yours
SUHANI 💗 💋
